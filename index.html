The Cottage

The cottage is approx 60 sqm with two bedrooms each with one double bed (140 cm) and windows with blackout blinds. Living room, dining/kitchen area, bathroom with shower, double entrance and a small storage room. Terrace with garden furniture.

The cottage was built in the early seventies but renovated 10-15 years ago.

The cottage is located only few steps from the lake.

Located within a gated community.

Full 4G mobile network coverage.

A beautiful place to view the Northern Lights during dark hours.

Perfect location when traveling in the south /south-west of Iceland.
