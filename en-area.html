Location
Bláskógabyggð, Iceland

The cottage is located at Lake Þingvellir.

The cottage is very well located for various sightseeing and day tours, e.g.

- 10 km from Þingvellir National Park

- 17 km Nesjavellir (geothermal power station)

- 44 km Kerið (volcanic crater lake)

- 65 km Geysir, hot spring / geothermal area

- 72 km Gullfoss, (waterfall)

- 85 km Reykholt, Borgarfjörður (partly gravel road) (historical site, Deildartunguhver (hot spring))

- many hiking trails in the neighborhood. Handbook with trails around Þingvellir is available in the cottage (in Icelandic)

Getting around

- 45 km from Reykjavik City center

- 90 km from Keflavik Intl Airport

- 30 km Mosfellsbær (shops, restaurants, swimming pool)

- 34 km Laugarvatn (grocery store, bath/pool)

- 55 km from Selfoss (shops, restaurants, swimming pool)


We recommend that our guests have transport of their own. There is no public transport in this area.
